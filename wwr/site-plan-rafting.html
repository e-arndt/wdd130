Rafting Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

To provide inforation about guided water rafting adventure trips for beginners to the most adventurous thrill seekers.

Audience

Who are the target customers? What do they want? The target audience will mainly consist of adventurers seeking a thrilling experience. Typically in groups, such as families, friends or corporate teams, but individuals and couples are welcome too.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Sight visitors will be able to see the equipment used and the types of adventures trips available for booking.

How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? The sight will be accessible using desktop or laptop computers and mobile devices.
